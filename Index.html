Frequently Asked Questions

Where are your hospitals located?
Sahayog Hospitals is currently in Gondia (Maharashtra), Gurugram (Haryana), and Nagpur (Maharashtra), and is expanding to more cities including Pune and Chandrapur.
Do you provide 24x7 emergency services?
Yes, our Emergency & Trauma Department operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week across all our hospital locations. We are always ready to provide immediate care.
How can I book an appointment with a doctor?
You can book an appointment by calling our reception numbers, visiting the hospital front desk directly, or through our website using the Book Appointment option. Online appointments are available and recommended for faster service.
Can I book consultations online?
Yes, online appointments are available via our website. You can browse our doctor panel, select a specialist, and choose a convenient time slot for either an in-person or virtual consultation.
Do you provide second opinions for medical conditions?
Yes, you can consult our experts for a second opinion. Please share your medical reports and history when booking, and our specialists will provide a thorough review.
Do you accept walk-in patients without prior appointments?
Yes, walk-in patients are welcome, especially for emergencies. However, prior booking is recommended for routine consultations to ensure quicker service and minimal waiting time.
